Our Work

How did the Sternenko Community Foundation begin? Do you use public donations to cover the Foundation's expenses? Can I become a volunteer of the Foundation? How can I be sure my donation is used as intended? Where can I see the results?

How did the Sternenko Community Foundation begin?

The Sternenko Community Foundation is a logical continuation of our volunteer efforts. For years, our community has been raising billions of hryvnias to supply Ukraine’s frontline with FPV drones, naval drones, and other essential equipment.

As the scale of support expanded, we realized the need for a more structured and efficient approach. That’s why we created the foundation — to mobilize additional resources, build lasting partnerships, and respond faster to the needs of our defenders. Since then, our impact has only grown.

Do you use public donations to cover the Foundation’s expenses?

No. Every donation made for the army goes directly to the frontlines and gets converted into FPV drones and other equipment urgently needed by the Defense Forces. We don't use donations intended for our defenders to cover our salaries or office costs.

Our operational needs are covered separately through dedicated contributions from partners and private donors.

If you'd like to support our ongoing work, you can support our current operations here.

Can I become a volunteer of the Foundation?

We love hearing from people who want to help. While we don’t have an open volunteer program, we’re always open to meaningful collaboration. However, if you have unique skills that can significantly strengthen our work — whether it’s logistics, tech, communications, or anything else — we’d love to hear from you. Reach out to us at appeal@sternenkofund.org

How can I be sure my donation is used as intended? Where can I see the results?

We believe in full transparency. Every day, we publish updates from units that receive our drones, including photo and video reports, including footage of drone strikes, so you can see your support in action.

We also release detailed monthly reports where every purchase and delivery is included. You can find all of them on our website and social media.

Visit the reports section on our website to see how every donation helps destroy enemy targets and save Ukrainian lives.

Helping the Military

What kind of support does the Foundation provide? Does the Foundation assist civilians? I’m a soldier. Can I request support without an official request from my unit? How can I request FPV-drones? How long does it take to process a request?

What kind of support does the Foundation provide?

We supply frontline military units with FPV drones — highly effective means for eliminating enemy equipment, personnel, and even russian drones and UAVs. We also assist selected units with gear for special operations at sea and behind enemy lines.

Does the Foundation assist civilians?

Not at this time. Currently, we focus exclusively on supporting the Ukrainian Defense Forces with FPV drones and equipment.In the future, we may expand our mission, but for now, our defenders remain our top priority.

I’m a soldier. Can I request support without an official request from my unit?

Yes — but there are important conditions. We do provide drones without formal paperwork from military units, but only when we’re confident the unit truly needs them, knows how to use them, and will use them effectively.

We also require sufficient reports. Units that receive drones must confirm delivery and demonstrate their use. If a unit does not report, we won’t continue providing support. This helps to ensure transparency, efficient use of resources, and the maximum possible impact on the battlefield.

How can I request FPV-drones?

You can submit a request by emailing us at apply@sternenkofund.org.

Please include the following information:

  1. Call sign, rank, unit, brigade
  2. Location/region where your unit is currently operating
  3. How long you’ve been working with FPV drones
  4. Whether you require drones to strike ground or aerial targets
  5. If any neighboring units are already working with our Foundation
  6. Your name and a phone number we can use to contact you via Signal

How long does it take to process a request?

Usually, we respond quickly — within minutes or a few hours.War doesn’t take days off, and neither do we. We do everything we can to get drones to the frontline as fast as possible. We’ll handle all the logistics so you can focus on what matters the most — defeating the enemy and protecting our people.

Donations & Support

How can I support you from abroad? How will my donation be used? Can I donate to specific requests or projects? Is it possible to return a donation if it was made by mistake?

How can I support you from abroad?

You don’t have to be in Ukraine to help.We accept donations in multiple currencies, via PayPal, and even in cryptocurrency.You can find all available options here.

How will my donation be used?

Every donation made for the military goes directly toward FPV drones and essential equipment for Ukraine’s defenders. None of these funds are used for the Foundation’s operations or salaries — every donation supports the soldiers on the frontline.

If you’d like, you can also support our fund’s daily operations (like logistics and coordination) through a separate donation here.

Can I donate to specific requests or projects?

Yes. Our website features active fundraisers and key projects you can choose to support.

Just select where you’d like your donation to go, and we’ll make sure it’s used exactly where it’s needed most.

Is it possible to return a donation if it was made by mistake?

Yes. If you made a donation by accident or entered the wrong amount by mistake, please contact us, and we’ll do our best to help you.

To help us process your request faster, let us know which platform you used (PayPal or any other method).

You can reach us at appeal@sternenkofund.org

Cooperation with Drone Suppliers

I’m an FPV drone manufacturer. Can the Foundation purchase drones from me? Does the Foundation need components for FPV drones?

I’m an FPV drone manufacturer. Can the Foundation purchase drones from me?

Yes, we’re open to working with new manufacturers — but only if you offer innovative solutions that haven’t yet been widely used on the battlefield.

We’re not looking for standard, off-the-shelf FPV drones that are already being mass-produced. If you’re developing something unique that can turn the tide on the battlefield, we’d love to hear from you.Contact us at prod@sternenkofund.org

Does the Foundation need components for FPV drones?

No, we don’t manufacture drones ourselves.When we need components, we already have trusted sources for them.

Partnership & Collaboration

How can a business or company support the Foundation? Can we organize a charity event together? Is it possible to become a long-term partner of the Foundation? Does the Foundation work with international organizations and companies?

How can a business or company support the Foundation?

We welcome collaborations and deeply value support from businesses. Companies can get involved in a variety of ways — through direct donations, joint initiatives, or custom partnerships. We offer flexible formats and are always open to new ideas and creative collaborations.

If you’d like to be part of our mission, reach out to us at business@sternenkofund.org — we’ll find the best way to work together.

Can we organize a charity event together?

Absolutely. We’re open to co-hosting charity events that support of the Foundation.If you’re interested in organizing one, contact us at business@sternenkofund.org — we’d love to discuss the details and explore possible formats.

Is it possible to become a long-term partner of the Foundation?

Yes, we’re actively looking for committed partners who can support us on a regular basis. This includes both businesses and international organizations. If you're interested in building a long-term relationship, please contact us at business@sternenkofund.org

Does the Foundation work with international organizations and companies?

Yes, we collaborate with international partners and are always open to new opportunities that strengthen our mission. If you represent an organization or company that wants to join our efforts, we’d be glad to explore how we can work together. Get in touch via business@sternenkofund.org
